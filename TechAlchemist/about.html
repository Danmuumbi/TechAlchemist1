Our Origin Story

From Frustration to Engineering with Purpose

In 2024, Samuel Kilonzo (CEO & Principal Architect) , Daniel Muumbi (CTO) and Kisamwa Samuel (Creative Director) founded TechAlchemist Solutions with a singular mission: to bring real engineering discipline to software development. After years delivering complex projects for global industry leaders, they saw how shortcuts and technical debt held businesses back instead of moving them forward.

The name "TechAlchemist" reflects our philosophy: we blend the art of design with the science of engineering to transform complex challenges into robust, scalable solutions. Unlike ancient alchemists who tried to turn lead into gold, we use proven principles of computer science, systems design, and industrial engineering to create real, measurable value.

What began as two engineers tackling complex software projects has grown into a full-spectrum engineering practice. Today, TechAlchemist Solutions designs not only custom websites and portals but also structured networks, secure server setups, advanced CCTV and security systems, and resilient digital brands supported by strategic marketing and SEO.
